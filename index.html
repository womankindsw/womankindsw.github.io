Discover a unique blend of wisdom and wellness in our Navigating Menopause in the workplace talks, hosted by a dedicated Wellness Coach and a Nutritional Therapist.

We deliver an interactive experience where light movement harmonises with science-backed knowledge, creating a space for empowerment and education.

The key topics addressed are based on menopause and andropause symptoms. We adjust the talk depending on the attendees and include any specific requests.

Talk structure

Our talks cover the six main topics below and are designed for active participation. Attendees walk away with practical tools to integrate into their daily routines.

The information shared can be used to support loved ones as well as colleagues in the workplace.

Other key components include mindfulness practice, exercises in the form of light physical movement as well as nutritional insights:

  1. Brain fog: Understand and combat the challenges of brain fog through nutrition and lifestyle adjustments.
  2. Bone strength: Explore strategies to support and enhance bone strength.
  3. Skin elasticity: Discover tips for maintaining healthy skin during this phase.
  4. Hot flushes/night sweats and sleeping: Navigate the complexities of hot flushes, night sweats, and sleeping patterns with practical solutions.
  5. How to support others: Receive guidance on how to introduce holistic menopause support, covering emotional and physical well-being.
  6. What to reduce and eliminate: Gain insights into lifestyle factors and dietary choices that can be adjusted to relieve menopausal symptoms.

Post-talk resources: Following the session, a handout will be forwarded to the organiser to be distributed within the company. This comprehensive document includes the main points discussed during the talk, along with any additional information requested by attendees. It is your go-to guide for continued well-being.
